About Me

I am currently studying full-stack development with Learning People. I am competent writing HTML 5 and CSS 3, and am able to implement Bootstrap 4 in web projects. I also have some experience using languages such as JavaScript, PHP, Asp.Net, and am currently learning to use Python. I have used frameworks such as jQuery and .Net. Additionally, I have some experience with web hosting and relational databases (SQL Server).

My professional experience in software development has so far consisted of writing VBA macros for a large organisation to manage significant volumes of emails, and maintaining a database recording feedback of customer service agents. My experience in web development has come primarily through maintaining my personal website, through which I promote and sell my novels.
